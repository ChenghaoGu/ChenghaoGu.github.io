Research Interests

My research focuses on Embodied AI, 3D vision, and spatial reasoning for robots. I am especially interested in:

  • Spatial understanding for vision–language–action models.
  • 3D reconstruction and simulation for robot learning.
  • Automatic task and dataset generation for manipulation benchmarks.

In short, I want robots to not only see the world, but also understand where things are, how they move, and how to interact with them safely and reliably.
